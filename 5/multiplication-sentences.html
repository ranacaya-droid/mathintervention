Detectives, we have a case! The baker, Mr. Gable, found 4 boxes of donuts on his counter. He knows each box has 3 donuts, but he needs to know the total number of donuts fast! How can we help him solve this case?

Connecting the Clues

When we have *equal groups*, we can use repeated addition (like 3 + 3 + 3 + 3) to find the total. Multiplication is a shortcut for this! 4 groups of 3 is the same as 4 x 3.
